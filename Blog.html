The Great Escape: How Music Clears My Mind

Posted on September 29, 2025

There's nothing quite like dropping a pair of headphones on and letting the world melt away. For me, the intense, pulsating energy of **EDM Music**—the Dubstep, DNB, and RAVE —is more than just noise; it's a therapy for me.

The moment the heavy bass and intricate rhythms hit, I feel like my problems just *go away*. The intensity of the music demands your full attention, drowning out mental clutter and stress, offering a momentary, powerful escape. It allows me to reset, recalibrate, and come back to reality with a clearer mind.

"Where words fail, music speaks."

Whether I'm coding or chilling, these tracks are my mental refresh button. Here are three go-to genres for instant headspace:

  1. EDM (for high energy, especially DNB)
  2. HIPHOP (for pure rhythmic groove and thought-flow)
  3. SAD Music (for a balanced, calming groove when the intensity is too much)

The Worlds Within: Why I Read Manga and Manhwa

Posted on September 25, 2025

For pure escapism and storytelling, nothing beats **reading manga or manhwa**. It's an incredible hobby that lets me dive into rich, fantastical worlds, far removed from the constraints of web development.

Whether it's the beautiful *line work* of a classic shonen manga or the vibrant, full-color panels of a Korean manhwa, the artistry is truly inspiring. It reminds me that even in technical fields, creativity is key.

"If you don't take risks, you can't create a future" (Monkey D. Luffy, One Piece),"

I am currently hooked on a fantasy manhwa that features incredible world-building. The dedication authors put into their craft is amazing, just like how we strive for perfectly structured HTML. Some genres I highly recommend are:

  • Isekai
  • Action/Comedy
  • Fanstasy
